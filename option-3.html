Questions

Is reformer Pilates suitable for beginners?

Yes. We run dedicated Beginner Group Classes, and every class is capped at four people so your instructor sets your springs, straps and pace personally.

How much do classes cost?

A 3 Class Pack is £69, with 5 and 10 Class Packs also available — both include a £5 discount code to share with a friend. Single classes and gift vouchers are available too.

How do I book?

Through our Momence booking page or the Momence app. For a specific private session time, email info@lalapilates.co.uk and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Where is the studio and is there parking?

We're inside House of NAF at 19 Elmbank Street, G2 4PB. There's on-street parking on Elmbank Street, Charing Cross Car Park is 2 minutes away, and Charing Cross Station is a 3-minute walk.

Do you offer private sessions?

Yes — private sessions for 1:1, 1:2, 1:3 and 1:4. The whole studio to yourself, ideal for injury recovery, nerves, or training with friends.

Is Pilates good for back pain?

Reformer Pilates builds the deep core and postural strength that supports the spine. With only four people per class, every exercise is adapted to you — mention any injury when you book.
