faq

good questions, straight answers

is reformer pilates suitable for complete beginners?

Yes. La La Pilates runs dedicated Beginner Group Classes, and every class is capped at four people so your instructor can set your springs, straps and pace personally. Private sessions are also available if you'd rather start one-to-one.

how much do classes cost?

A 3 Class Pack is £69, with 5 and 10 Class Packs also available — and when you buy a 5 or 10 pack you get a £5 discount code to share with a friend. Single classes, private sessions and gift vouchers are all booked through Momence.

how do i book a class?

Book online through our Momence booking page or the Momence app. For a specific private session time, email info@lalapilates.co.uk and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

where is la la pilates located?

Inside House of NAF at 19 Elmbank Street, Glasgow, G2 4PB — in the city centre near Charing Cross, with easy access on and off the M8.

is there parking or a train station nearby?

There's on-street parking on Elmbank Street and Charing Cross Car Park is a 2-minute walk (check parking charges). Charing Cross Station is 3 minutes on foot, Glasgow Central and Queen Street are a short walk, and Cowcaddens and St George's Cross Subway stations are close by.

is pilates good for back pain?

Reformer Pilates is widely used to build the deep core and postural strength that supports the spine. With a maximum of four people per class, exercises are adapted to you — tell us about any injury when you book.
